Cheeseburger

Description

This recipe will teach you how to make an incredible double bacon cheeseburger.

Ingredients

For this recipe you'll need the following:

  • Ground Beef
  • Salt
  • Pepper
  • Bacon
  • Cheese (Any kind will do)
  • Burger Buns
  • Tomatoes
  • Lettuce
  • Onions

Steps

  1. Make your bacon, doesn't matter if you fry it or bake it, it's up to personal preference
  2. Make two small balls with your groud beef
  3. Heat the pan, we reccomend using a cast iron pan over high heat
  4. Open a window, it's gonna get smoky!
  5. Place your patties into the pan and and use a spatula or another pan to smash them until they're about 1/4 of an inch thick
  6. Cook patties until they're that signature patty color
  7. Take the patties out and turn off the stove
  8. Assemble your burger and enjoy!
