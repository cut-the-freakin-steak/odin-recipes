Lasagna

Description

This lasagna recipe will teach you how to make a mouthwatering lasagna with 10 layers, cheese, beef, and tomato sauce!

Ingredients

For this recipe you'll need the following:

  • Pasta/Tomato sauce
  • Ricotta cheese
  • Mozzarella cheese
  • Parmesan cheese
  • Beef
  • Sausage
  • Garlic
  • Egg
  • Lasagna
  • Onion
  • Spices
  • Fresh Basil Leaves (Optional)

Steps

  1. Boil the Lasagna Pasta
  2. Prepare meat sauce with sausage, onion, and garlic
  3. Mix up all of the cheeses together
  4. Layer the Lasagna Pasta, meat sauce, and cheese mixture together 10 times
  5. Put it into the oven or the air frier until the top of the lasagna is golden brown
  6. Take it out of the oven and let it rest for at least 15 minutes (and up to 45 minutes)
  7. Enjoy!
