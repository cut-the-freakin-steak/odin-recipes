Pizza

Description

This pizza recipe will teach you how to make a deliciously simple pizza with just some tomato sauce, storebought (or homemade, but that won't be covered in this recipe) pizza dough, cheese, vegetables of your choice, and meats of your choice!

Ingredients

For this recipe you'll need the following:

  • Tomato sauce or crushed tomatoes
  • Mozzarella cheese (Any will work but mozzarella is best for this)
  • Pizza dough (Homemade or storebought)
  • Vegetables of your choice (Ex. Olives, Bell Peppers, Onions, get creative!)
  • Meats of your choice (Ex. Pepperoni, Sausage, Pork, etc.)

Steps

  1. Prepare your dough (If homemade, make your dough from scratch)
  2. Open your tomato sauce can
  3. Knead the dough and make it into a circular shape, around 10-12 inches in diameter
  4. Bake the crust in a 500 degree preheated oven for 5 minutes
  5. Take dough from oven and spread your tomato sauce all over the surface
  6. Add your cheese, veggies and meat (In that order!)
  7. Bake again, this time for 8 minutes, or until the crust is golden brown
  8. Slice and enjoy!
